Visualization of the ratings

Below graph visualizations of the answers to the scalar questions (e.g. ratings 1-7), boolean questions (e.g. yes/no), and categorical questions (e.g. type of religion).

Filter Options

Select a button on the left to filter the answers.

Show All Shows all questions in their original order.

Show Most Agreed Upon Sorts questions on most similarly answered (e.g. everyone gave rating 'accepted').

Show Most Controversial Sorts questions on most differently answered (e.g. all rates are equally often given).

Show Most Positive Sorts questions on most positively rated (e.g. questions with the largest amount of rating accepted).

Show Most Negative Sorts questions on most negatively rated (e.g. questions with the largest amount of rating rejected).
